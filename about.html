History of MATE

  • 2013
    The Beginning

    Manish Patel and Ravish Patel, both passionate about trading, started exploring the financial markets, gaining hands-on experience in stock and forex trading.

  • 2015
    Early Struggles & Learning Phase

    During this period, they faced multiple challenges and losses in trading. Determined to master the markets, they deep-dived into finance, technology, and trading strategies.

  • 2017
    Breakthrough in Trading

    After years of research and experience, they developed a strategy focused on risk management and minimizing losses, leading to consistent profits.

  • 2019
    Sharing Knowledge with Traders

    Realizing the need for structured trading education, they started mentoring traders and small investor groups, refining their teaching approach.

  • 2021
    Developing a Unique Trading Philosophy

    They introduced the "Zero Trading Loss" philosophy, emphasizing smart trading strategies, risk management, and capital protection.

  • 2023
    Foundation of MATE

    MATE (MultiAsset Traders Education) was officially established, aiming to revolutionize trading education through practical learning and technology integration.

  • 2024
    Expanding Influence

    MATE rapidly gained recognition, helping traders master various asset classes while integrating fintech solutions for real-time trading analysis.

  • 2025
    Global Recognition

    With a growing community of successful traders, MATE is now recognized as a leading institution in trading education and financial literacy.
